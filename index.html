Understanding Radon Risk

Key facts every Edmonton homeowner should know — plus trusted Canadian resources for deeper reading.

Why Edmonton Homes Are at Risk

Radon is a radioactive gas that forms naturally in soil and rock across Alberta. It enters homes through foundation cracks, sump pits, and utility openings — often without any visible sign. Because you cannot see, smell, or taste radon, professional testing is the only way to know your exposure level.

Health Canada recommends action when indoor levels reach 200 Bq/m³. Many properties in the Edmonton region exceed this guideline.
